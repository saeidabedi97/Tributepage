Biography

Majid Samii is an Iranian neurosurgeon and medical scientist.
He has been awarded many times as a best neurosurgeon in the world.

    Timeline of Prof.Samii's life:

  • 1937:Born in Tehran, Iran.
  • 1950:Finished highschool.
  • 1951:Moved to germany.
  • 1970:Recieved PHD of neurosurgeon.
  • 1977:President of neurosurgeon deparmtent, Hannover, Germany.
  • 1997-2001: President of international neurosurgeon organization
  • 2004:President of Milad's hostipal, Iran
  • 2008-present:Created a hostipal for neurosurgeon, Rasht, Iran
    Awards
  • 2006: Iranian Science and Culture Hall of Fame.
  • 2008: Award of the People's Republic of China.
  • 2010: Society of America Medal Recipient.
  • 2013:Leibniz-Ring-Hannover Prize winner.
  • 2013: Elected by the World Neurosurgery journal.
  • 2014: World top neurosurgeon and Golden Neuron Award Winner.
